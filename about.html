About US


            Glory Tabernacle church is a ministry that is set to make a mark in our generation, infesting and to spearheading the works of the kingdom of God in our community and beyond. We have an assignment and that is, the great commission to reach out to the unsaved, disciple them to know their God and to begin to do great exploit (Dan 11:32 ). GTC is a church that has been helping turn hopelessness into hope in people's lives across the globe.
This, which is being done in a real, visible and tangible form in our day was inaugurated by the Lord Jesus Christ and will continue till His return.
The Apostolic-prophetic ministry carries the anointing (not to break) but to destroy the devil’s yoke, securing breakthroughs which God intends. Healing for the nations and peoples cannot be achieved until the government of God is forcefully asserted.
TRAINING and IMPARTING spiritual gifts and blessings to God’s people so that they can effectively serve the purpose of God anytime anywhere in this crucial end time hour.

And

To lay hold of our inheritance through Christ, now and by FORCE!

A ministry commissioned by God to demonstrate the Apostolic-Prophetic Glory of God, to declare the oracles of God and to assert His sovereign authority in these End Times as Moses, Elijah and John the Baptist did in their generation. The preaching of the gospel that brings SALVATION and SOLUTION to people of all nations, tribes and tongues. Unleashing God’s Word without fear or favor in this final moment of the last days without excuse, apology or compromise. This is our commitment.
The Apostolic-prophetic ministries (Ephesians 2:20) of the last days is the end time plan of God for the church to establish the authority of God through the power of the Holy Spirit. To delete, erase and exterminate the works of the devil and to erect the purposes of God.
"I have this day set thee over the nations and over the kingdoms, to root out, to pull down, and to destroy, and to throw down, to build, and to plant." (Jer.1: 10)

To lay hold of our inheritance through Christ, now and by FORCE!
