Vission And Mission

Ignite a true and lasting smile from your heart.

Our Vision Commitment


            That the great commission to witness the Lord Jesus Christ to the world was given to the church -- all believers. It is God’s command to give the Gospel to all mankind. To fulfill the great commission it is necessary for every local church to train and send evangelists and missionaries and to reach out to souls and plant new churches to disciple them. (Mk. 16:15; Acts 1:8; Rom 10:13-15; 2 Cor. 5:19, 20). To achieve this we are committed to Missions/Evangelism, Church planting, discipling.

Mission Statement


            “Overrun the works of darkness”
“For the earth shall be filled with the knowledge of the glory of the LORD, as the waters cover the sea” (Habakkuk 2:14).
