Our Founder

Ignite a true and lasting smile from your heart.

The General Overseer


            Pastor George OhiaAfter receiving the Lord Jesus as His savior in a very strange circumstance has had several supernatural encounters, which have helped shape his life and spiritual walk with God. After graduating in B.A. Economics, he further attained two degrees in Law. As a registered legal practitioner, he had a strong desire to become a notable lawyer fighting the cause of the underprivileged. Nevertheless, he yielded to the call of God. Since then he has been engaged in various areas of ministry. He has served twice as the General Overseer of the Victory Fellowship International aka A.I.I.C.S.F, with branches in several countries. He also pastored at Jubilee Christian Church, Durhan, North Carolina.
Pastor George has taken various theological and biblical trainings. He was the rector of Berechiah Bible College, Pune and a lecturer at Bethsaida Bible Training College.
George has pastored for several years and he also ministers at various conferences, seminars and crusades. He believes in a dynamic apostolic-prophetic end-time church.
